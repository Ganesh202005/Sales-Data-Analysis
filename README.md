# 📊 Mobile Sales Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a mobile sales dataset to identify key trends, patterns, and business insights related to product performance, customer behavior, and revenue generation.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📂 Dataset Features

The dataset includes:

* Product & Brand information
* Price and Quantity Sold
* Customer details and location
* Region-wise sales
* Inward and Dispatch dates
* Technical specifications (RAM, SSD, etc.)

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

### 📈 Sales Analysis

* Total revenue and sales trends over time
* Monthly performance and seasonal patterns

### 📦 Product Analysis

* Top-selling products and brands
* High revenue vs low-performing products

### 🌍 Regional Analysis

* Revenue distribution across regions and locations
* Identification of high-performing markets

### 💰 Pricing Insights

* Relationship between price and quantity sold
* Impact of product specifications (RAM, SSD) on revenue

### 🚚 Operational Insights

* Delivery time analysis using inward and dispatch dates

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Sales show clear seasonal trends with peak periods in specific months
* A small number of products contribute significantly to total revenue
* Higher-priced products tend to have lower sales volume
* Certain regions consistently outperform others in revenue generation
* Product specifications such as RAM and SSD influence customer purchasing behavior
